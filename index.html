Your Gateway to Northern Wonders

A Destination Where Heritage Meets Progress

Experience the vibrant spirit of Candon City, where every corner holds a story and every vista inspires. We invite you to explore a destination that perfectly balances deep-rooted traditions with modern adventure.

From the architectural majesty of the city center to the serene horizons of our coastal escapes and greenery forests. Your northern journey starts here.

Favorite Spots

Candon City Arena

A world-class indoor facility and the premier venue for sports, culture, and major events in Ilocos Sur.

Eco Park

A lush green sanctuary perfect for nature lovers, offering scenic walking trails and a peaceful escape from the urban bustle.

Candon City Aquarium Park

Explore marine biodiversity at this modern leisure destination, featuring diverse aquatic species and beautifully landscaped grounds.

Tamurong Beach

Discover the serene coastline of Candon, where golden sands meet the West Philippine Sea, ideal for surfing and relaxation.

Candon City Plaza

The historic heart of the city, honoring local heroes and serving as a vibrant gathering place for cultural festivities.
